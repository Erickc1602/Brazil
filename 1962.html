Brazil 1962

Squad

Gilmar, Djalma Santos, Mauro Ramos de Oliveira, Zózimo, Nílton Santos, Zito, Didi, Garrincha, Mário Zagallo, Vavá, Amarildo

Man Of The Tournament

Garrincha

Garrincha. Nicknamed the “Little Bird,” no player flew higher in Chile. When Pele was knocked out of the tournament with an injury, it was the Brazilian winger, renowned for his mazy dribbling skills, who hoisted the team on his shoulders

The Final

Brazil 3 vs 1 Czechoslovakia
